What Is The Internet?

The internet is a global network of computers that are connected to each other and can share information using a common set of communication rules called protocols.

Think of the Internet as a highway, and the cars on the highway is all the information, websites, and browsers. The internet connects all of these things and makes it easy navigate from one to another.
