How Does The Internet Work?

The Internet works by connecting different networks of computers through a system of routers.

What Happens When You Use It

You connect to the network system and are able to access information and communicate with other computers.

Major Components

    Servers: Store websites and data. Routers: Direct traffic between networks. ISPs: Internet Service Providers connect users to the global network. Protocols: Rules that govern data exchange, like TCP/IP and DNS.

Why It Matters

The Internet has transformed education, communication, and business. It enables social connection, supports global collaboration, and powers everything from email to streaming to online learning.
